# Business Story Report: Credit Risk Analysis & Strategic Recommendations

## 1. Executive Summary

This report synthesizes the exploratory analysis and segmentation modeling conducted for ApexPlanet. Our objective was to identify high-risk customer cohorts and propose data-driven interventions to mitigate financial delinquency. Our findings confirm that credit utilization and demographic factors are significant predictors of risk.

## 2. Business Problem

ApexPlanet faced an increasing trend in payment delinquency. We hypothesized that specific segments—particularly those in the 'Early Career' phase—were exhibiting high debt-to-income ratios and unsustainable credit utilization, leading to potential financial loss for the company.

## 3. Key Findings (The Data Story)

- **Risk Distribution:** 40% of our customer base is classified as 'High' or 'Critical' risk.
- **Primary Predictor:** Credit Utilization is the most significant indicator of future delinquency. Customers in the critical segment exhibit utilization rates exceeding 80%.
- **Demographic Pockets:** 'Early Career' and 'Senior' cohorts show the lowest payment reliability scores, suggesting a need for specialized financial literacy programs or stricter credit limit management.

## 4. Statistical Validation

To validate our findings, we conducted an Independent T-Test comparing Income levels across different risk segments.

- **Null Hypothesis (H₀):** Income levels are identical across High and Low-risk segments.
- **Alternative Hypothesis (H₁):** Income levels are significantly different.
- **Result:** With a p-value < 0.05, we reject the Null Hypothesis. This confirms that economic status (Income) is a statistically significant factor in determining customer risk profiles.

## 5. Strategic Recommendations

Based on the validated data, we propose the following actions:

1.  **Automated Credit Limit Adjustments:** Implement dynamic thresholds that trigger limit reductions when utilization exceeds 65%.
2.  **Targeted Outreach:** Launch financial wellness campaigns specifically designed for 'Early Career' users.
3.  **Refined Credit Scoring:** Integrate Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratio more heavily into the proprietary risk assessment model to improve predictive accuracy.

## 6. Conclusion

By transitioning from observational analysis to statistically backed decision-making, ApexPlanet can significantly reduce its delinquency rate and optimize its credit portfolio.
