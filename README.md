# ApexPlanet Data Analytics Internship - Task 4

Welcome to the repository for Task 4 of the ApexPlanet Data Analytics Internship. This task focuses on **Data Storytelling and Statistical Validation**, bridging the gap between raw data analysis and business-critical decision making.

## 🚀 Task Overview
In this task, we transform the exploratory insights from Tasks 1-3 into a compelling narrative for stakeholders and validate these findings using rigorous statistical methods.

## 📊 Key Activities
- **Statistical Validation:** Performed independent T-tests to prove that customer risk segments have statistically significant differences in income and credit profiles.
- **Data Storytelling:** Crafted a business report summarizing risk drivers (e.g., credit utilization, age group trends).
- **Strategic Recommendations:** Proposed data-backed interventions to mitigate credit delinquency.

## 📁 Repository Structure
- `Task_4_Presentation.pdf`: The finalized pitch deck for business stakeholders.
- `business_story_report.md`: A detailed report on the business problem, insights, and recommended actions.
- `Hypothesis_Testing_Summary.md`: Documentation of the statistical methodology (T-Tests) and validation results.
- `analysis_code.ipynb`: The Jupyter Notebook containing the data wrangling, segmentation, and T-test calculations.

## 📈 Methodology
To ensure robustness, all insights were validated using:
1. **Hypothesis Formulation:** Defining null and alternative hypotheses for risk segment income.
2. **T-Testing:** Calculating p-values to ensure findings are not due to random chance.
3. **Data Narrative:** Synthesizing complex findings into actionable business KPIs.

## 💡 How to Run
1. Ensure your environment has `pandas`, `scipy`, and `matplotlib` installed.
2. Load your `segmented_dataset.csv`.
3. Run the `analysis_code.ipynb` notebook to reproduce the statistical validation results.
